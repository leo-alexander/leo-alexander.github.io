Daily Mindfulness

Chapter Three of Chade-Meng Tan's Search Inside Yourself introduces the concept of Mindful Conversation, of which there are three components:

    Mindful Listening: An exercise in which one person speaks uninterrupted for three minutes, while the other listens. Aftwerwards, the two have a conversation about their experience.
    Looping: Where feedback is given to acknowledge that a person has understood what the other has said.
    Dipping: Where the listener is responding to internal distractions by acknowledging them.

I will try to use Looping and Dipping more often from now on.
