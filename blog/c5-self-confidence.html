Self Confidence

This week continued our read of Search Inside Yourself with Chapter 5.

A part of achieving mindfulness is self-awareness. Self awareness is summarised as:

  • Emotional Awarenesss: Recognizing one's emotions and effects
  • Accurate self-assessment: Knowing one's strengths and limits
  • Self-confidence: A strong sense of one's self-worth and capabilities

I tried one of the two practices to achieve self awareness - Body Scan, which brings attention to each part of the physical body.
