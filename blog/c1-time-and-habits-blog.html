Time and Habits

I consider time management (or lack thereof) to be one of my major fallbacks. Thankfully, Dev Academy has provided a repo of useful time and habit management resources for students to work though and put in to practice while they learn and work.

I was particularly interested in the concept of “flow”. Essentially, it’s when you’re “in the zone”, and you’re maximising the efficiency and quality of your work, without distraction or writers block. I’ve decided that throughout this course I will be working towards gaining the ability to get into a state of flow more often. This will involve fulfilling a few certain criteria, and a bit of trial and error. We’ll see how that goes but I’m feeling optimistic.
