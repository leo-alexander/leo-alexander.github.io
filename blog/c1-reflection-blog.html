Reflections: Dev Academy

As I write this, I’m at the end of the first week of Enspiral’s Dev Academy. For the first half of this course, we are learning from home. This confirms to me that Dev Academy is technologically up to date, and progressive in nature. I feel at home in this kind of environment - I can see my self working easily here either in teams or solo. So far, the working environment of EDA is as expected. The curriculum is what I’ve already learnt previously (I expect this to change soon). Still, I am learning heaps just through practise - one of the benefits I see in EDA. It takes learning a step further from using online resources by creating an environment where you are able to put them into practise with other students. I’m certainly excited and nervous about the next few weeks. Excited, because I know I already love programming and can’t wait to see what I can create! Nervous because, as with my fellow students, I’ve invested a lot into this course ie. time and money. It’s scary!
